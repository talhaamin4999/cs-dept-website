Eligibility Criteria

🎓

BS Program

FSc Pre-Engineering or equivalent with minimum 60% marks. Matric with at least 55%.

📊

Entry Test

University NTS/Entry test covering mathematics, logical reasoning, and English proficiency.

📝

MS Program

BS CS or related field with minimum 2.5 CGPA (out of 4.0). GAT General with 50+ score.

🔬

PhD Program

MS in CS or related field with 3.0+ CGPA. Research proposal and interview required.

Application Steps

01

Create Account

Register on the university admissions portal with a valid email address.

02

Fill Application Form

Complete the online application with academic history and personal information.

03

Upload Documents

Submit scanned copies of transcripts, CNIC, passport photo, and experience letters (if any).

04

Pay Application Fee

Pay PKR 2,000 via online banking or at the university cashier.

05

Appear for Entry Test

Attend the scheduled entry test at designated test centers.

06

Merit List & Enrollment

Check merit lists online and complete enrollment within the specified deadline.
